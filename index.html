What to notice

How to read each view

Phasor: the FFT bin is a backward-spinning reference. A carrier only appears still when its spin exactly matches bin k. Any leftover spin traces a circle.
3D matrix: tall accent bars on the diagonal mean clean OFDM. Gold/red side bars are ICI. Drag the canvas to orbit.
Spectrum: each curve is one carrier's sinc; dashed blue lines are FFT-bin centres. Clean OFDM places samples on every other carrier's zeros.
Constellation: raw vs equalised. Red error vectors point from received to ideal QPSK target.
